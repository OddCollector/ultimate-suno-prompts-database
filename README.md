# 🎵 Ultimate Suno Prompts Database

A comprehensive, organized database of music prompts for Suno AI music generation. Features an interactive, expandable interface for easy browsing and prompt discovery.

## Features

- 📚 **24+ Categories** - Comprehensive coverage of all music production aspects
- 🎨 **Expandable Interface** - Click any category to expand/collapse
- 🔍 **Searchable Database** - Quickly search for specific prompts
- 📋 **Copy to Clipboard** - Click any prompt to quickly copy it
- 🎯 **Well-Organized** - Prompts grouped by music theory, production, and creative elements
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** - Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript, no dependencies

## Categories Included

1. **Structure & Arrangement** - Intro, Hook, Chorus, Verse, Bridge, Outro, etc.
2. **Genre** - Electronic, Hip-Hop, Pop, Rock, Jazz, Classical, etc.
3. **Vocal Type & Style** - Tone, Texture, Effects, Multiple Voices
4. **Mood & Emotion** - Happy, Sad, Angry, Calm, Romantic, Mysterious
5. **Atmosphere** - Spacious, Intimate, Bright, Dark, Ethereal
6. **Instruments** - Piano, Guitar, Strings, Woodwinds, Brass, etc.
7. **Drums & Percussion** - Acoustic, Electronic, Breakbeat, Jazz, etc.
8. **Production** - Lo-Fi to Hi-Fi, Analog to Digital
9. **Effects** - Reverb, Delay, Distortion, Vocoder, Auto-Tune, etc.
10. **Music Theory** - Chords, Keys, Scales, Harmony
11. **Rhythm & Tempo** - Time Signatures, Swing, Syncopation
12. **Sound Design** - Synthesis Types, Sampling, Foley
13. **Lyrics & Themes** - Love, Heartbreak, Personal Growth, etc.
14. **Song & Album Artwork** - Design Styles & Concepts
15. **AI Video** - Music Video Concepts & Formats
16. **File Naming Conventions** - Organization Standards
...and more!
